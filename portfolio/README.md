## What Makes a Great Portfolio?

### Essential Elements
- **About section**: Who you are and what you do
- **Projects**: 3-5 of your best work with descriptions
- **Skills**: Technologies you know
- **Contact info**: How people can reach you

### Nice to Have
- Blog or articles
- Testimonials or recommendations
- Resume/CV download
- Links to GitHub, LinkedIn, etc.

## Design Tips for Beginners
- **Keep it simple**: Clean and minimal beats cluttered
- **Use good fonts**: Stick to 2-3 fonts max
- **Color scheme**: Pick 2-3 colors and use them consistently
- **Show, don't tell**: Screenshots and demos speak louder than descriptions
- **Mobile-first**: Make sure it looks good on phones

## Suggested Tech Stack

### For Beginners
- HTML, CSS, JavaScript

### Intermediate
- React or Vue.js

- Netlify or Vercel for hosting

### Advanced
